Fourier Transforms

Real line

For f\in L^1(\mathbf{R}) define the Fourier Transform \hat{f}(t) = \int_{\mathbf{R}} f(x)\exp(-2\pi i x t)\,dx.

If \hat{f}\in L^1(\mathbf{R}) then f(x) = \int_{\mathbf{R}} \hat{f}(t)\exp(2\pi i x t)\,dt.

\int_{\mathbf{R}} \exp(-\alpha x^2)\exp(-2\pi i x t)\,dx
